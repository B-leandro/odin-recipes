Description

Pasta stand for meals as carbon stand for life. Pasta is life, Pasta is carbon. Don't doubt the power of shapes, textures and subtle variations in ingredients.

It's easy to cook pasta since you buy the thing already made and just put it in hot water. So, what you need to do that?

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • a 'lil' bit of salt

Steps:

  1. Put water in a pot
  2. Put pot on hot hob
  3. Let water reach the boiling point
  4. Add salt and mix
  5. Add pasta, drop the hob knob. You don't need full power when the water is already boiling, just keep it there
  6. Stir a couple of times
  7. Take one piece of pasta and taste it, not always the cook time is perfect
  8. If it suits your taste, drain the water and serve
